Our Culinary Destinations

France

Discover the art of French living through its refined gastronomy

• French cuisine was added to UNESCO's World Heritage List in 2010

• The French consume over 30,000 tons of cheese annually

• Paris has more than 40,000 restaurants, the highest density in the world

Japan

Explore the delicacy and precision of Japanese cuisine

• Japanese cuisine emphasizes seasonality with over 200 different types of seasonal ingredients

• Tokyo has more Michelin-starred restaurants than any other city

• Traditional Japanese meals follow the "ichiju-sansai" principle: one soup and three dishes

Italy

Savor the authenticity and passion of Italian cuisine

• Italy has over 600 different pasta shapes, each with its own history

• The first pizza was created in Naples in 1889 for Queen Margherita

• Italians consume about 25kg of pasta per person annually

Thailand

Discover the harmony of flavors in Thai cuisine

• Thai cuisine balances five fundamental tastes: sweet, sour, salty, bitter, and spicy

• Street food is so integral that it's recognized as part of Thailand's cultural heritage

• Thailand is the world's largest exporter of rice, with over 3,500 varieties
